# 💸 Salary Tracker : The "Freedom Countdown" Edition

Welcome to the only app that makes watching paint dry look productive by comparison. This is a high-precision, low-stress financial instrument designed to answer the eternal question: _"Have I earned enough for a fancy coffee yet?"_

## 🚀 Features

- **Real-time Wealth Generation**: Watch your net worth grow by 0.001 TWD increments. It’s not much, but it’s honest work.
- **The Freedom Clock**: A countdown timer that tells you exactly how much longer you have to pretend to be busy.
- **Mood-Adaptive Waves**: The background changes from "I love my job" Green to "Get me out of here" Orange as the day progresses.
- **Anti-Stutter Technology**: Waves so smooth you'll forget you're actually at a desk.
- **PWA Ready**: Install it on your desktop so you can stare at it instead of your spreadsheets.

## 🛠 Installation (The "I'm a Developer" Way)

1.  **Clone the vibes**:
    `git clone https://github.com/yourusername/salary-tracker.git`

    Just double-click `index.html`. No `npm install` madness. No 500MB node_modules. Just pure, unadulterated HTML.
3.  **Profit**: Literally.

## 📖 How to Use

1.  **Input your shift**: Tell the app when the suffering starts and ends.
2.  **Input your Daily Wage**: Be honest. Or don't. Lie to yourself for a morale boost.
3.  **Stare intently**: Research shows that staring at the counter makes time move 15% slower, but the satisfaction of seeing the numbers go up is worth it.
